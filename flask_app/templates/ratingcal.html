Rating Calculator

This tool allows you to calculate the rating you would receive for a given difficulty constant and score. If the score field is left empty, it will display the S to SSS+ ranks by default. Additionally, you can use this utility to determine the difficulty levels you would need to play in order to achieve your desired rating.
